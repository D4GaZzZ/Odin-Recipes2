Tonkatsu Chiken Recipe

About this dish,i think we could say the same as the ramen,but this time i think is maybe to expensive for a student...

Ingredients:

  • Chicken: You'll need four skinless, boneless chicken breast halves.
  • Salt und pepper
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Panko
  • Oil

Step by Step

  1. Season the chicken, then dredge in flour.
  2. Coat each breast in egg, then press into the Panko
  3. Fry the chicken katsu until golden brown

Special Sauce
